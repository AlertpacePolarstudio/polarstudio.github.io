About Us

Polarstudio is a small indie game studio focused on atmospheric horror games. Founded in 2025, our mission has been to create immersive worlds where tension and storytelling lead the experience. We combine eerie visuals, haunting sound design, and psychological tension to bring unique horror journeys to life.
